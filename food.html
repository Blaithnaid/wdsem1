Food

Kai Cafe

This Michelin Guide Cafe, named after the Maori word for food, will provide you with high- quality food all sourced locally.

Wa Cafe

Wa cafe, also know as Wa Sushi, serve Japanese sushi, and have vegan-friendly items on their menu. It is definitely considering a a staple of Galway.

Dough Bros Pizza

One of the best pizza places in Galway, if you're looking for something tasty and simple, this is the perfect joint for you.

Zappis Galway

Have a craving for authentic Italian food but unsure where to even start looking? Zappis, based in the heart of Galway city is just the place to go.

Asian Tea House

Looking to dine on some delicious Asian food? Or perhaps you'd like a few cocktails with your friends? Well Asian Tea House offers both, with their own Buddha Bar right inside!

Tuco's Taqueria

Awarded the title "Best Mexican" by the Food Awards Ireland, Tuco's is sure not to disapoint your tastebuds.
